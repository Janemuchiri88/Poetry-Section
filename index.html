I obeyed every command

He wrapped his arms around me,
I could feel his heart chugging,
But this was a melody to my ears,
His love was all that mattered to me,
Through his brown eyes,
I could see his passion.

Wind's caresses

As we walked down the beach,
we held hands as our feet were,
immersed in the sand,
The chilly water slowly greeted our toes,
And te cool wind caressed my bare back.

He made me weak

His touch sent chills down my spine,
His gentle kisses around my neck,
made me weak, His fire ignited and rose the beast,
I couldn't hold back anymore,
I obeyed every command.
